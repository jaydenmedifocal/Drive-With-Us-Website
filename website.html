What to expect from your first lesson

Your first lesson is all about setting you up for success. We'll start with the basics in a calm, supportive environment, so you can ease into the driver's seat with confidence. Here's what you'll need to get started:

  • A valid learner's permit.
  • A logbook (if you're under 25).
  • Comfortable clothing and shoes for driving.

During your lesson, we'll cover:

  • Adjusting your seat, mirrors, and steering wheel for optimal control.
  • The fundamentals of starting, stopping, and steering.
  • An introduction to parking and lane changes—no pressure, just practice.

Manual Driving Lessons

For those who prefer greater control over their driving experience, manual driving lessons are an excellent choice. Learning to drive a manual car means mastering gear shifting and understanding the mechanics of the manual transmission.

Our manual driving instructors will teach you the necessary skills to drive a manual vehicle confidently. From foot off the clutch to moving the gear stick into first gear, we'll guide you through every aspect of driving a manual car, ensuring you gain full control over your vehicle.

Advantages of Manual Transmission

  • Better Control: Manual vehicles offer greater control over the car, especially in situations like hill starts and braking.
  • Fuel Efficiency: Manual cars tend to be more fuel-efficient, especially in older models.
  • Cost-Effective: Manual cars are typically cheaper to buy, maintain, and repair compared to automatic vehicles.
Book Lesson

Automatic Driving Lessons

Automatic driving lessons are perfect for those who prefer a simpler driving experience. With automatic transmission, you don't need to worry about operating the clutch or shifting gears, making it easier to focus on steering, braking, and traffic awareness.

Our automatic driving lessons are ideal for new drivers. Whether you're learning in an automatic car or simply want a more relaxed driving experience, our instructors will ensure you are fully prepared to drive an automatic vehicle with confidence.

Advantages of Automatic Transmission

  • Ease of Use: Automatic transmission allows you to drive without worrying about gear changes, making it simpler, especially in heavy traffic.
  • Convenience: With no clutch to manage, you can focus on steering and road conditions, perfect for urban driving.
  • Great for Beginners: Automatic cars are often easier to learn, making them a popular choice for those just starting out.

The Safer Drivers Course is designed to teach learner drivers to reduce risks and anticipate hazard situations by improving decision-making on the road.

In Class

Module 1: THEORY

This module engages learner drivers in an interactive session designed to explore critical driving strategies and behaviours through dynamic discussions and activities.

INCLUDES

  • Three hour facilitated group discussions
  • Videos and activities to keep the sessions engaging
  • Explore behaviours and strategies that lead to becoming a safer driver
  • Includes: hazard management, influences, speed management, and more.

In Car

Module 2: PRACTICAL

This in-car practical component provides a two-hour coaching session where learner drivers practice key driving skills on a set route, focusing on eliminating common crash risks and enhancing hazard recognition.

INCLUDES

  • Two hour driving coaching session with 1 other student
  • Each drive is aimed at eliminating major crash types on SA roads
  • Multiple guided drives on set routes
  • Includes: responding to hazards, checking your personal compass, and more.

Completed a minimum of 50 actual logbook hours

Hours must be real driving hours and not 3 for 1 bonus hours.
