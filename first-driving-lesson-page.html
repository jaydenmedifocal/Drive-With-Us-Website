Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about learning to drive with Drive With Us.

What should I bring to my first lesson?

For your first lesson, you'll need to bring your learner's permit, comfortable clothing and shoes, and a positive attitude! We'll provide everything else you need to get started.

How long does it take to learn to drive?

The time it takes to learn to drive varies for each person, but most students are ready for their test after 20-30 lessons. Our structured approach helps you progress efficiently and build confidence along the way.

Can I choose my instructor?

Yes! We understand that having the right instructor can make all the difference. We'll work with you to find an instructor who matches your learning style and schedule.

What if I'm nervous about driving?

It's completely normal to feel nervous about driving! Our instructors are experienced in working with nervous learners and will help you build confidence at your own pace. We start with the basics and gradually increase the complexity as you become more comfortable.
