The Drive With Us Method

Our unique teaching method is built on the fundamental principles of safe driving: space and vision. We teach you to maintain safe distances from other vehicles and to scan the road ahead for potential hazards.

This approach not only helps you pass your driving test but also prepares you for a lifetime of safe driving on Australian roads.

Our instructors are trained to adapt this method to your individual learning style, ensuring you build confidence and competence at your own pace.

Drive With Us has been operating since 1969, making us one of Australia's longest-running and most experienced driving schools. Over the past 55+ years, we've helped over 400,000 students become safe, confident drivers.

Our unique Drive With Us Method focuses on the fundamental principles of safe driving: space and vision. We also provide dedicated instructors, personalized coaching, and a proven track record with a 71% first-time pass rate.
