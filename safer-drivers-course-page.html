What is the Safer Drivers Course?

The Safer Drivers Course is designed to teach learner drivers to reduce risks and anticipate hazard situations by improving decision-making on the road.

The Safer Drivers Course is for learner drivers in SA under the age of 25, with a minimum of 50 logbook hours, to develop their safe driving skills through theoretical and practical training. Upon the successful completion of the course, they will receive an extra 20 log book hours.

Course Details

Our Safer Drivers Course is designed to help you become a safer, more confident driver while earning valuable logbook hours.

3 Hours in Class

Learn about hazard perception, risk management, and defensive driving techniques in a classroom setting.

2 Hours in Car

Practice the skills you've learned in a real driving environment with our experienced instructors.

20 Bonus Hours

Receive 20 bonus logbook hours upon successful completion of the course.

Course Eligibility

To be eligible for the Safer Drivers Course, you must meet the following requirements:

Age Requirements

  • Must be under 25 years of age
  • Must hold a valid learner's permit

Logbook Requirements

  • Must have completed at least 50 logbook hours
  • Must be a South Australian resident
